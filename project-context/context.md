# Project Context Prompt: Aegis Risk Group Website

## Project Overview

Create a professional corporate consulting website for **Aegis Risk Group**, a Bangkok-based consulting firm specializing in **insurance strategy, corporate liability reduction, enterprise risk advisory, claims prevention, and executive protection from legal and financial exposure**.

The website should feel serious, premium, strategic, and trustworthy. The brand should communicate protection, discipline, foresight, and corporate defense. The tone should be executive-level, suitable for C-suite decision makers, board members, corporate legal teams, insurers, and enterprise clients.

The visual direction should follow the existing Aegis Risk Group identity: **black, white, and dark bloody red** as the core color palette. The design should feel modern, militaristic, and consulting-oriented without becoming too aggressive or gimmicky.

---

# Brand Identity

## Company Name

**Aegis Risk Group**

## Brand Positioning

Aegis Risk Group helps corporations identify, reduce, transfer, and manage risk before it becomes legal, financial, or reputational damage.

The company acts as a strategic defense partner for organizations operating in complex regulatory, operational, and insurance environments.

## Core Brand Keywords

- Protection
- Liability reduction
- Risk defense
- Corporate resilience
- Insurance intelligence
- Strategic advisory
- Claims prevention
- Executive foresight
- Bangkok-based expertise
- Regional corporate risk knowledge

## Visual Style

The website should use:

- Dark black or near-black backgrounds
- White typography for contrast
- Deep blood-red accents
- Clean section spacing
- Strong editorial layouts
- Corporate photography
- Grid-based layouts
- Sharp geometric dividers
- Subtle shield, line, fortress, or tactical map motifs
- Premium consulting firm feeling

Avoid making the website look like a generic insurance agency. It should feel like a high-level risk strategy and liability defense firm.

Aegis Risk Group is a Bangkok-based corporate risk and insurance advisory firm focused on helping organizations protect themselves from preventable financial, operational, legal, and reputational exposure.

Our role is to identify these exposures early, reduce liability through better systems and documentation, and structure insurance programs that match the real risks of the business.
